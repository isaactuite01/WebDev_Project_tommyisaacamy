About Lucas Snow

Lucas Snow is a professional skier and snowboarder. He was born in the snowy peaks of Colorado, where he honed his skills on the slopes and developed his profound connection to the mountains. Sponsored by Redbull, he hs travelled to over 20 countries, showcasing his talent and winning various competitions; namely the Snowboard World Cup and securing a gold medal in the 2022 Winter Olympics.


Why Snow Trail Travels?

Acting on his love for snow sports, Lucas has created a travel agency, Snow Trail Travels to bring his love for snow sports to the masses.
